Our guarantee

The piece-count promise

The biggest fear in puzzle swapping is receiving a box with missing pieces. Here's exactly how we handle it — from the moment you list a puzzle to the moment something goes wrong.

1

You list a puzzle

Scan the UPC, add a photo of the box and the assembled puzzle. You certify the piece count is complete.

2

Matched & packaged

We match you with a swap partner. You use our Welcome Kit mailer and pre-paid label to ship.

✓

You confirm receipt. Both accounts are credited. The swap is complete.
